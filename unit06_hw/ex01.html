About Me

I'm a creative web designer & developer based in Warwickshire, UK. I love crafting dynamic, engaging websites using the latest browser technologies, as well as designing for alternative platforms such as mobile devices and Facebook.

I've worked in conjunction with some of the biggest brand names in the UK, including Next, Very, Play.com, Sky, The AA, and more.

You came here for the technical details? I specialise in HTML5, CSS3, JS & jQuery and PHP & MySQL, but can also be found fulfilling clients needs tinkering with other languages and frameworks such as .NET & MSSQL, PhoneGap and Magento. Throw the Adobe design suite in to the mix for good measure. At the moment I'm fascinated by the inner workings of responsive web design and client-side preprocessors.

When away from the desk you might catch me playing and watching football, ,kicking back on my Xbox or relaxing with my family.
